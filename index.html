Born in Chalissery, Palakkad district,Abhi’s formal training began with a 5-year National Diploma in Mural Painting from the Guruvayur Institute, where he graduated with first-class honours. Over 20 years, he has mastered diverse styles including mural painting, modern art, texture art, abstract art, graffiti, minimalist art, 3D art, and cement relief wall art. Abhi is an artist with a natural flair for art and an unquenchable passion to dream beyond boundaries. Recognizing art as the canvas for his life’s journey, he embarked on a path that would define his legacy.

Sustainable Artistry: The Use of Natural Colors

Abhi is deeply committed to preserving the authenticity and sustainability of traditional art forms. He meticulously extracts pigments from natural sources such as stones, leaves, and minerals, ensuring that each piece of art is not only visually stunning but also environmentally responsible. This practice reflects his reverence for nature and his desire to honor the rich artistic heritage of Kerala, where natural dyes have been used for centuries in mural paintings and other art forms.

By using these organic materials, Abhi creates works that are timeless, vibrant, and imbued with the essence of the earth. His dedication to this craft underscores his belief that art should harmonize with the environment rather than exploit it.

Global Aspirations: Bridging Cultures Through Art

He seeks to share his unique blend of traditional and contemporary art with a global audience. His work has already garnered acclaim in Dubai, where he continues to push creative boundaries by blending mural art with abstract aesthetics.

With aspirations to collaborate with artists, institutions, and communities worldwide, Abhi envisions his art as a bridge between cultures. His ability to seamlessly fuse Kerala’s artistic traditions with modern techniques positions him as a true global ambassador for sustainable and culturally resonant art.

MOOD BOARDS

<

These mood boards serve as visual blueprints for my creative process, where Kerala's rich cultural heritage meets contemporary minimalism. Each board is a thoughtful synthesis of textures, colors, and motifs that narrate a story—bridging the past and the future. From intricate temple carvings to bold geometric forms, these collages encapsulate the essence of inspiration and guide the transformation of ideas into large-scale murals that evoke emotion and cultural resonance.

The juxtaposition of elements reflects my signature style: abstract expressionism intertwined with cultural storytelling. Earthy tones like ochre and terracotta anchor the compositions, paying homage to Kerala’s landscapes, while modern accents such as neon highlights and metallic textures introduce a contemporary edge. The interplay of rough graffiti tags with smooth 3D cement reliefs creates a dynamic tension, embodying the harmony of chaos and order. These mood boards are not merely sketches but strategic frameworks for crafting art that is both timeless and boldly original.
